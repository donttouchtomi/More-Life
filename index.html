ABOUT ARTIST

Central Cee also known as 'Cench', is a british rapper from shepherd's bush, london. His style blends together elements of hip-hop and Uk Drill. He first saw success when he appeared alongside J Hus, Bankaz and many more on the Ain;t On Nuttin Remix in 2015. He has since gone on to release a series of viral songs, such as 'Day in the Life' and 'Loading' in 2020
