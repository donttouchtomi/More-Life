ABOUT ARTIST

Solána Imani Rowe (born November 8, 1990), better known by her stage name SZA, is a singer-songwriter. Rowe was born in St. Louis, Missouri but raised in Maplewood, New Jersey. She released her self-released debut EP, See.SZA.Run in October 2012.
